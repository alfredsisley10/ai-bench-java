Need access? The WebUI delegates auth to an upstream identity proxy (oauth2-proxy / SSO gateway) via X-Forwarded-Email. Locally, aibench.auth-enabled=false grants every request the Manager role automatically. To grant Manager rights in a deployed instance, add your email to aibench.admin-users in ~/.ai-bench/bench-config.yaml and restart the WebUI.

Operating the banking app: Click Start banking app to launch ./gradlew :app-bootstrap:bootRun in banking-app/; output streams to banking-app/tmp/bootRun.log. The status badge auto-refreshes while STARTING. If the badge shows ERROR, View log for the stack trace — most failures are Spring bean wiring (missing @Autowired on a multi-constructor service) or a stale process holding port 8080. See the operations guide for login flow, full button reference, and troubleshooting recipes.
